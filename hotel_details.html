    About this area

    Located in Toronto, Chelsea Hotel is in the city centre and on the waterfront. The area's natural beauty can be seen at High Park and Yonge-Dundas Square, while Ripley's Aquarium of Canada and Ontario Place are popular area attractions.

    About this property

    Along with 2 restaurants, this smoke-free hotel has a full-service spa and an outdoor pool. Free WiFi in public areas, a free airport shuttle and free train station pick-up are also provided. Other amenities include 4 bars/lounges, a 24-hour health club and a poolside bar.
